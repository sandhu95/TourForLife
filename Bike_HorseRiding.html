About Paint Ball

What is Paint Ball?

Paintball,It is a competitive team shooting sport in which players shoot their oppnents with a spherical paintball.It can be played indoor and outdoor.Depending upon the variant played, this game can last from minutes to hours.

what safety rules should be followed in this game?

  • Safety goggles must be worn at all times.
  • Blind firing is not allowed.
  • No shooting or dry firing in the neutral area or in the parking lot.
  • Barrel covers are required.
